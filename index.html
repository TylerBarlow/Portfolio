Who is this

My name is Tyler. I'm a student expanding my knowledge at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver. I don't believe in pixel-perfect design. I believe pixel perfection is not attainable nor useful. I aim to use iteration and metrics to continuously (and relentlessly) move towards a better experience. I enjoy my work most when I'm pushed to rapidly learn new tools, perspectives and methods to move forward. Thus far, my education has exposed me to skills in web development, Arduino, processing, UX and UI design. I'm excited to show you what I have done, and I'm excited to add more.
